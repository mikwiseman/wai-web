Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

WaiVPN is operated by WaiWai, LLC. We built this VPN to protect your privacy, not to collect your data. This policy explains exactly what we do and do not collect.

No Activity Logging

We do not log your browsing activity. We do not record which websites you visit, what content you access, or what DNS queries you make. There are no activity logs, period.

No Traffic Monitoring

We do not inspect, monitor, or store the contents of your VPN traffic. Your data passes through our servers encrypted and is never analyzed, recorded, or shared.

Minimal Data Collection

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the service:

  • Invite code — used to activate your account. We store a hashed version to verify your access.
  • Connection timestamps — we may record when a connection was established or terminated for diagnostic purposes. These are retained for no more than 72 hours and contain no information about your traffic or destinations.
  • App version and platform — collected during subscription sync to ensure compatibility.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

Crash Reporting

WaiVPN uses Sentry for crash reporting. When the app encounters an error, an anonymized crash report may be sent. These reports contain:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App version
  • Stack trace of the error

Crash reports do not contain your IP address, browsing data, VPN traffic, or any personally identifiable information. Sentry data is used solely to fix bugs and improve the app.

No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your data with any third party. We have no advertising partners, no analytics trackers, and no data broker relationships.

Data Retention

Diagnostic connection timestamps are deleted within 72 hours. Crash reports are retained for 90 days. Invite code hashes are retained for the lifetime of the account.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of any data we hold about you. Since we collect almost nothing, there is very little to delete. Contact us and we will process your request promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, contact us at hi@mikwiseman.com.
